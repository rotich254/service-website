About Us

At Arban, we are revolutionizing access to essential home services in Kenya. As the country experiences rapid growth with both local and foreign entrepreneurs building their dream homes, the demand for reliable, affordable services like plumbing, electrical work, gardening, moving, catering, and cleaning has never been higher. Unfortunately, these services are often difficult to find and expensive. Arban is here to bridge that gap by partnering with trusted companies to offer a wide range of home services at affordable prices while ensuring top-tier customer experiences. Our mission is to connect customers with service providers quickly and easily through our user-friendly online platform, where services can be booked via calls, emails, or messages and delivered seamlessly. We aim to elevate the service industry by making it more approachable and convenient, addressing the scarcity of essential yet often overlooked services such as TV installation, outdoor furniture assembly, and toilet replacement. At Arban, we're committed to making home service solutions accessible to everyone in Kenya.
